The primary antagonist or perpetrator in the story is Jonathan Small. He is described as a poorly educated, small, active man with his right leg amputated, wearing a wooden stump. He is middle-aged, sunburned, and has a history of being a convict. The crimes involved a hidden treasure and the death of Major Sholto, one of the characters in the story. The missing treasure and the pursuit of justice drive the plot, with Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson working to unravel the mystery and bring the perpetrators to justice.
